Outdoor Dining Information Collector

  • This map is created to serve as a tool to record information about restaurants in Tacoma, WA.

  • Due to the pandemic, not all restaurants provide indoor dining. However, there are some restaurants that provide outdoor dining service. People can pick a point for restaurants and put their information to share.

  • This map will only be used as an inventory of restaurant infomration in Tacoma.

    • Method:

    1. Pick a location for the restaurant of your choice by using the marker button on the left side of the map.

    2. Put any infomration about the restaurant in the pop-up screen.

    3. If you made a mistake, use the trash can button under the marker button to delete the point.
