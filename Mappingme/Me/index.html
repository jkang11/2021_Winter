Mapping My Life in Tacoma.

This map is created to show my life in Tacoma, WA. I moved to Tacoma December 2020 from South Korea. I used to live here, but it's been awhile since I left. How have I lived so far? Well... because of the virus, I haven't really got to travel around the city. This map shows where I like to visit and go for grocery shopping, and also places that I want to visit!

It's funny that most of my go-to places are not in Tacoma, but my home is in Tacoma, so... it should be good, right?

To navigate the map, I added some functions. You will be zoomed to any location that you click. If you click on markers, the pop-up will open to show the information. Also, you can pick any two points to find directions by driving, walking, and cycling. To remove the points, click the "X" button in the direction. Feel free to play with the map!
